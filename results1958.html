These are the results of the season 1958


This is the first season of the Formula Junior


The first season of the newly formed series of the Formula Junior is held in Italy. The series immediately goes international with one race in portugal and france. The whole season contained 11 races with only 6 of them counting for the Italian championship. This first season is dominated by Stanguellini's chassis and Fiat engines. First we show an overview of all races and results of the italian championship. Then the other races are displayed.
